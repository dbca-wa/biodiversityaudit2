The filtered view is an advanced, interactive data portal which allows to select the region (scale) and/or taxon (scientific of common name) and conservation status of interest to view our latest knowledge of ecological details, threats and management actions about a taxon in a region. Be aware that data come from a read-only source and any changes will be discarded. Do not use this interface to update data.
